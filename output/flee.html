Flee

By John Mathews
Category: General
Jul 04 2017


Summer children

squeal and shriek,

Watch wars.

Excitement mounts.


How exotic.


Unexpectedly,

Death arrives.


Grow-up

Grow old

Face death

Evade evil.


Don’t tire.


World is fragile.

Towers burn,

Bridges bludgeon,

Markets stab.


Got to get out.
